Project Details

Type: Location: Size: Category:
Office, Commercial Surat, Gujarat, 395007 1900 SQ. FT. Interior Design

Within the existing textile building, “Textile Haven” emerges—a transformation that transcends mere functionality. Here, every square meter is a canvas, and architecture responds with purpose.

The walls, capped at 2.1 meters, serve as our guiding light. Natural illumination penetrates the office’s deeper corners. The partition system, a delicate balance of veneer, fluted glass, and clear glass, ensures privacy without compromising openness. Each piece of furniture is an artful creation, meticulously designed to harmonize with the space. Moreover, in this symphony of functionality, a lounge space bridges boss cabins—a place where business conversations flow seamlessly into leisurely moments.

Step into the expansive display room—a textile lover’s paradise. Modular racks span the walls, displaying the latest fabrics and prints. Muted colors envelop the senses, inviting touch and exploration. Warm wood accents punctuate the palette, cocooning visitors in comfort. The same materials—wood grain, fabric weaves, and glass textures—reveal their multifaceted personalities. The space evolves as you move through it, each finish adding depth and definition.
