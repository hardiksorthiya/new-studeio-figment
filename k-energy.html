K-Energy Stations
Gujarat

Project Details

Type: Location: Size: Category:
Utility Gujarat, India 900-2500 SQ. FT. Architecture

K Energy redefines the petrol station experience with its distinctive design language that marries simplicity with modern elegance. The angular forms, combined with the station’s sleek lines, present a futuristic vision that is both practical and visually stunning

The color palette is a thoughtful choice, with orange as the main hue representing energy and enthusiasm. White adds a clean, minimalist backdrop, while red serves as a sophisticated secondary highlight, bringing depth and contrast to the design. Together, these colors elevate K Energy’s brand as premium and progressive, setting a new standard for refueling stations.
